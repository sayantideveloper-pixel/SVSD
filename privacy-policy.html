Privacy Policy:

SVSD has created this privacy policy to demonstrate its firm commitment to protecting your privacy. The following document describes our policies and practices regarding the collection, distribution and protection of information.

Compliance:

By entering into a contract with SVSD, you agree to the terms of SVSD's Privacy Policy described below:

Collection of information SVSD does not collect information about you or your organization other than what you have provided to us.

Use of information SVSD collects this information to make better decisions for mutual benefit, to support the activities you choose, and to provide higher quality training and services. We do not sell or rent this information to any person or organization for commercial purposes.

Information security SVSD classifies your personal information as confidential information. Confidential information is protected by storing it on secure servers that allow access only to SVSD staff and students. The data is encrypted to prevent unauthorized persons from accessing it.

Website Information Management Policy:

  • When your staff and students visit our website, we do not identify or collect IP address, browser, ISP, referring/exit pages, platform type, date/time, stamp, number of clicks, domain name or country/state.
  • We may use your IP address to diagnose problems with our server and to administer the site.
  • We will not use your information to improve our website without written permission from an authorized signatory of your organization.
  • Our website may contain links to third party websites. SVSD does not control and is not responsible for the content, privacy practices or reliability of such websites.

Change in the policy

SVSD reserves the right to modify or supplement this Privacy Policy at its sole discretion.
