Sri Tapan Kr. Pobi
The Founder President, SVSD

SET is a center for growth, innovation, and excellence in addition to being a place of learning. This institution offers a diverse range of courses, including engineering, architecture, and medical programs. We encourage innovation and problem-solving in the engineering discipline to better equip our students to meet the complex problems of the contemporary world. While our medical programs develop caring, knowledgeable healthcare workers, our architecture program promotes creative design thinking and sustainable solutions.

Swami Vivekananda once opined: “There is no other teacher but your own soul” and inspired by this vision of Swamiji, our students stay abreast with the ethics, ethos and ideologies of a completely integrated human being. They are taught to accept their own social and cultural responsibilities.

I urge our students to seize the opportunity that SET presents, to dream large, and to pursue excellence as we move forward. Together, we'll keep adding to the institution's success tales and change the world for the better.

Sri Shiba Prosad Datta
Chairman, SVSD

Welcome to SVSD!

Enjoy the journey toward a great future. Since your present shapes your future, we tend to sharpen your present capabilities so that you can emerge as a successful engineer or entrepreneur in the future. Present-day institutions must produce technically adept and bright graduates to be able to cope with the fast-changing world. Achieving holistic development of the students and education system is our utmost priority. Our students excel in all spheres of academia. Our main objective is to produce a whole bunch of robust graduates who shall, with full gusto, create benchmarks through their skills and ideas in core corporate sectors.

SVSD advocates equal, fair rights for education and an admission system for all. No discrimination, cheating, or unfair actions are meted out toward our students.

Mr. Suman Pramanik
PRINCIPAL, SVSD

As I start to pen my thoughts, a lot comes to my mind which I feel privileged to share with you all. At the outset, I express my gratitude to the parents who have put their faith in us to educate their wards. This brings with it tremendous responsibilities and I assure you that we are doing our best to live up to your trust in us.

Welcome to the Department of Diploma, Swami Vivekanda School of Diploma which was established in the year 2012. Our Diploma program is duly approved by AICTE and affiliated with West Bengal State Council of Technical and Vocational Education and Skill Development (Technical Education Division).

The Department of Diploma has an excellent infrastructure with a suitable atmosphere for Research and Development and highly qualified and dedicated faculty who are committed to providing an outstanding educational experience to the students. The faculty members contribute substantially to academia through quality teaching, publications, seminars, conferences, and many more.

Dr. Snigdhadip Ghosh
Registrar, SVSD

"One of the most powerful tools to empower individuals and communities is making certain that any individual who wants to receive a quality education can do so." With these objectives, Sanaka Educational Trust’s Group of Institutions is striving hard to provide one of the best 'Edu-Tech' supports to all students by means of the implementation of Outcome Based Education System with specific inputs from industry experts, interdisciplinary research of global importance, highly qualified faculty, and active industry-academia interactions supportive infrastructure for holistic development leading to a secured career. SVSD is an educational ecosystem, which consistently attempts to offer the best educational facilities to its students while evolving itself to the changing educational needs of the corporate world. We groom our students to develop into individuals with strong ethical values and also focus on enriching their social etiquette. SVSD aims to develop the highest quality minds, which are always questing for new ideas or inventions that can serve society. Apart from the academic facilities, the Institute strongly focuses on extracurricular activities to nurture its students. We impart the best of the knowledge to our students with unmatched extracurricular and emotional quotient-related inputs and the capability to acquire knowledge, lead and excel to make them responsible human beings
